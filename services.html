       New Construction

    • Bonfiglio Studio implements a collaborative method that engages clients and subcontractors early in the process. It considers not only the requirements of the client (needs and desires), and the site (zoning and building code), but also the best construction systems that will help deliver what the client wants within the appropriate time frame and budget constrains.

    For complex projects such as multi-family dwellings or mix-use developments, Bonfiglio Studio promotes Integrated Project Delivery methods, involving all participants through all phases of design and construction.
